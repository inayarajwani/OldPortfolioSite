Scratch Project 1


Instructions


  1. Press the flag key right beside the red stop button in the top right corner to start.
  2. After that, click on the red sprite. Then, click on the orange sprite.
  3. Once you have read the dialogue, press the a key on your computer, then the b key, c key, d key, e key, f key, g key, h key, i key, j key and finally the k key.
  4. You press these keys in order so that the dialogue makes sense and goes in order.
  5. Once you have pressed the k key, you have finished my story.
